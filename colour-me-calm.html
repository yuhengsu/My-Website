Colour Me Calm

Colour me calm is a colouring game where the user uploads a picture of their choice. Then the game will break down the picture into a grid where the user then has to colour each square by correctly matching the numbers in the square to the numbers in the colour palette. The size of the grid depends on the difficulty that the user selects and the difficulty goes from a range of easy to hard.
