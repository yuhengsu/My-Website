About Me

My name is Yu Heng Su, but I also go by Benson. I am currently a third year student at University of Toronto studying computer science and mathematics.

With the skills that I am learning, I hope to help make softwares that will either assist or entertain people. I started programming when I was in high school and have loved it ever since. I really enjoy the freedom that comes with programming, as you can really do anything you want with it. I think of it as a giant toolkit for building anything I want to build.

Some of my interests includes : hiking, cooking, eating good food and of course playing video games. One of my all time favourite video is Ori and the Blind forest, I personally fell in love with the story, the artwork and the soundtrack during my first playthrough.
