About Me

My official name is Yu Heng Su, but I also go by Benson. I am currently a third year student at the University of Toronto computer science and mathematics. I enjoy developing software that either assists or entertain others, with games as my preferred specialty and main interest. I enjoy programming and never see it as work. I love taking on new challenges and adapting to different styles and languages!

Some of my interests includes : hiking, cooking, eating good food and of course, playing video games. One of my all time favourites is Ori and the Blind forest. I fell in love with the story, artwork and the soundtrack during my first playthrough, and I hope to make a game just as elegant one day.
