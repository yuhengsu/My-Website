Hello

My name is Yu Heng Su, but people usually just call me Benson. I am currently a third year undergraduate student at the University of Toronto majoring in computer science and mathematics. I’ve always been fascinated by the possibilities of computer technologies as a child and have developed a particular interest in software development. Throughout my studies, I’ve enjoyed developing software that either assists or entertain users, focusing on games as my preferred specialty and primary interest.

In my time off I enjoy going for a hike, cooking, eating good food and of course, playing video games. One of my all time favorite games is Ori and the Blind Forest. I fell in love with the story telling, artwork, and soundtrack during my first playthrough. It is my goal to make a game just as enjoyable and elegant one day.

Programming is always fun for me and I never see it as just work. I’m always looking to take on new challenges and adapting to different styles and languages. In a world that is increasingly becoming digitized, I am excited by the possibilities of video games and its roles in the future, and I look forward to taking part in shaping the possibilities of this future.
